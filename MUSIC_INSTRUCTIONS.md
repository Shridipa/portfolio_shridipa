# Instructions for Adding Ambient Music

## Step 1: Find Royalty-Free Magical Music

Since I cannot include copyrighted Harry Potter music, please download a royalty-free magical/mystical ambient track from one of these sources:

### Recommended Sources:
1. **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com/music/)
   - Search for: "magical", "mystical", "fantasy", "ambient"
   
2. **Freesound** (https://freesound.org/)
   - Search for: "magical ambience", "fantasy music"
   
3. **YouTube Audio Library** (https://studio.youtube.com/channel/UC.../music)
   - Filter by: Ambient, Cinematic genres

### Recommended Search Terms:
- "magical ambient"
- "mystical atmosphere"
- "fantasy background music"
- "celtic ambient"
- "enchanted forest"

## Step 2: Add Your Music File

1. Download your chosen music file (MP3 format recommended)
2. Rename it to `ambient-music.mp3`
3. Place it in the `c:\Portfolio\public\` folder

## Step 3: Test

1. Refresh your portfolio at http://localhost:5173/
2. Look for the golden music button in the bottom-right corner
3. Click it to toggle the music on/off

## Features Included:

✅ **Toggle Button**: Golden circular button in bottom-right corner
✅ **Visual Feedback**: Pulsing ring animation when music is playing
✅ **Tooltip**: "Enable Ambient Magic" hint that appears for 5 seconds
✅ **Loop**: Music loops continuously while playing
✅ **Icons**: Volume2 (playing) / VolumeX (muted)
✅ **Smooth Animations**: Hover and click effects

The music will enhance the magical atmosphere of your portfolio!
